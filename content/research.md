Modern medical imaging generates massive amounts of complex data. My research focuses on developing robust computational approaches to extract meaningful clinical insights from these modalities, bridging the gap between raw data and actionable medical knowledge.

## Current Directions

### Deep Learning for Medical Image Segmentation
Accurate segmentation of medical images is a fundamental step in automated diagnosis and quantitative analysis. I apply state-of-the-art deep learning architectures to improve the precision, efficiency, and robustness of segmenting anatomical structures and anomalies. 

### AI-Powered Workflows in BME
Beyond pure algorithmic design, I am intensely interested in integrating cutting-edge AI technologies and large language models into biomedical research pipelines. By optimizing these computational frameworks, I aim to accelerate data processing and enhance the overall efficiency of biomedical engineering workflows.
